# qrsurvey
QR Code Survey System

This system will be designed to get a customer from a business 
like a mall or doctors office to scan an attractive poster
(with a mention of a chance to win a prize upon entry)
which will then introduce them to a brief form 
that will question their experience at the establishment.
Marking all feedback and storing it in a database
which can be presented to the client who's business the form was for

The steps on the form should be something like as follows
1. Welcome message/banner (inviting)
2. Question 1 (inticing; impossible not to click;
   all the questions should provide something enjoyable to the user
   (light up buttons, satisfying movement or animation as a result of progress;
   progress bar itself, giving gratification and something to look forward to)
4. Question 2 (the whole way through, tell them they are getting closer to the
   contest entry by however many questions)
5. Question 3 & 4
6. Contest entry (contact)
7. Thanks for completing the form and entering the contest
This is the general idea for the form layout itself.

poster design will be crucial but saved for another time.
